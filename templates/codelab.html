Dictionary Traversing (Loops)

Instead of position indices, standard dictionary traversal iterates through the Keys. You then use each key to access its corresponding value.
emp = {"ID": 101, "Name": "Amit", "Salary": 45000}

# Traversing through a loop
for key in emp:
    print(key, ":", emp[key])
